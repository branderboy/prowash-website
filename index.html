ABOUT PRO WASH

THE BEST IN HAND WASH

Pro Wash is a premier full-service hand car wash. Pro Wash offers the time efficiency of an express automated car wash with the quality and swirl free paint results of a hand wash.

We offer an array of service packages from basic hand wash to the extensive master detail package. We also offer consultation and education on basic paint and interior car care. Power washing services are also available and include decks, sidewalks, houses, fleet washing and dealership lot spraying.
