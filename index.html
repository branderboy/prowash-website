Our Mission

BUILT TO SET THE STANDARD IN AUTO CARE

Pro Wash is a premier full-service hand car wash franchise serving Maryland and Washington DC. We built this company on a simple belief: your car deserves better than a machine wash.

We deliver the speed and convenience of an express automated wash with the quality, care, and swirl-free results that only a trained hand wash team can provide. From basic exterior washes to full master detail packages, fleet washing to power washing services — we do it all, and we do it right.

Every Pro Wash location follows the same proven process, trained staff, and quality standards. That's the franchise promise.
